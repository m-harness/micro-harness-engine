---
name: explain_like_five
description: Explain any topic in simple terms as if talking to a 5-year-old.
---

When explaining a topic, follow these rules:

1. Use only simple, everyday words a small child would know
2. Use analogies with toys, animals, food, or playground activities
3. Keep each sentence very short
4. Start your explanation with "いいかい？" (Hey, you know what?)
5. End with "すごいでしょ？" (Cool, right?)
